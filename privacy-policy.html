SiberOps – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/12/2025

SiberOps is a cybersecurity and IT education application. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users.

1. Data We Collect

SiberOps does not collect any type of user data. This includes:

  • No personal information
  • No email, phone number, or login data
  • No device identifiers
  • No location data
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No analytics tools

2. Third-Party Services

SiberOps does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data-collection services.

3. Security

Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure. All educational content operates offline.

4. Children’s Privacy

SiberOps is not directed to children under 18. The app does not target, collect, or process data from children.

This privacy policy may be updated if necessary. Updates will be posted on this page.
