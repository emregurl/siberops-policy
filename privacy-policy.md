# Privacy Policy for SiberOps

SiberOps does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

## Information Collection
The application operates fully offline and does not require a user account or login. No analytics, personal identifiers, or usage data are collected.

## Data Storage
All content is stored locally on the device. No information is sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services
The app does not use any third-party SDKs, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks.

## Security
Since no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of exposure of personal information.
